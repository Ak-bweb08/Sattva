I’m Dr. Yashika Nathani
Your Psychiatrist

Dr. Yashika Nathani, M.B.B.S, MD, is a board-certified psychiatrist and a practicing clinician with a certificate of additional qualifications in Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (CBT) from Beck Institute and Perinatal mental health and De Addiction psychiatry from NIMHANS, Bengaluru.

She is also involved in research related to adolescent problems, suicide prevention, and mental health awareness among the general population and also modalities like mindfulness & meditation.

She is trained in comprehensive and integrated management of patients with conditions like depression, schizophrenia, alcohol and other substance use disorders, bipolar mood disorder, obsessive-compulsive disorder, and many others. She also has expertise in incorporating different therapies like art, breathing, muscle relaxation alongside counseling, pharmacotherapy , psychosocial management with family therapy.
